About The Washington State FGC

The Washington State Fighting Game community is a large and robust one. We play a huge variety of fighting games, from the most popular and competitive, to the silly, bad, and forgotten relics. We believe fighting games are best experienced offline, so we strongly encourage everybody to use the resources and calendar provided here to find locals they can go to and a community they can be a part of. If you want to engage with us online, the PNW Airdashers discord is a great place to find people and info. It's mainly focused on anime fighting games (Guilty Gear, Under Night, Granblue, etc). If you are interested in Street Fighter, Tekken, or something else, check out the links in the Resources section below!


You can also find specific locations and their relevant discords and TOs. The Seattle fighting game scene is the largest and most active. But, you can find awesome local communities in Spokane, Pullman, Olympia, Bellingham and anywhere else people live here.
